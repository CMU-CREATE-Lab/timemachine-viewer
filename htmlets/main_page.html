    Plant Growth & Early Universe Updated

    07/15/2011

    The Plant Growth dataset has been updated. In addition to 6 more days of footage, the time machine is also now rendered at full resolution, allowing for an increase in zoom detail. A few visual defects have also been corrected.

    The Early Universe dataset has also been updated. It has been renamed to Evolution of the Universe and includes several hundred additional frames.
